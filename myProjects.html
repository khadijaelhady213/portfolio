01 TELEGRAM BOT

The bot consists of an application that is able to make comments through Telegram and registers them in Google sheets automatically. The aim is to replace the traditional method of making phone calls to stores and write them down on paper.

So basicly the customer has the options to see the products and to carry out the order choosing the day on which he will go to pick them. The order will end up in the seller's Google sheets along with the user id code, name, phone, date.
